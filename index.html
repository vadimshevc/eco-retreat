Sustainable Tiny Homes

Eco-friendly tiny homes built with natural, sustainable materials.

Sustainable Materials

These homes feature high insulation values and are produced using sustainable and/or natural materials whenever possible. One of the standout features is the 'rounded corners,' made possible by the specific material choices. Building with wood offers numerous advantages: it's lightweight, flexible, well-insulated, and allows for fast, efficient construction.

Innovation

Our goal is to continually develop and innovate so that these homes become 100% sustainable and circular. A key priority is ensuring they remain affordable while utilizing solar energy, wind power, and the best insulation.

Wood

The load-bearing structures are made of wood, a sustainable and eco-friendly material.

Finish

The load-bearing structures are made of wood, ensuring durability and sustainability.

Steel Frame

The steel frame provides strength and the necessary rigidity.

Glass Wool

The combination of glass wool with a wooden frame structure offers excellent insulation.

Triple Glazing

Triple glazing, combined with glass wool and a wooden frame, ensures superior insulation and energy efficiency.

Floor Heating

The floor is heated using infrared technology, powered by solar energy.
